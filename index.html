Patient Data Collection App

Voice Recording

Click "Start" to begin recording your voice. Click "Stop" to stop the recording. You can download the recording as a WAV file.

Handwriting Spiral

Use your mouse or touch input to draw a spiral on the canvas. Click "Clear" to erase the drawing, and "Download" to save it as an image.

Accelerometer Data

Click "Start Accelerometer Data Recording" to begin recording accelerometer data. Click "Stop Accelerometer Data Recording" to stop the recording. You can download the recorded data as a CSV file.
